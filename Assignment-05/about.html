Arnold Lobel

Anrold Lobel was an American author of the childrens books, including the Frog and Toad series and Mouse Soup. He both wrote and illustrated those picture books, as well as Fables, for which he won the 1981 Caldecott Medal reconizing the years best illustrated U.S. picture book.

He was born in Los Angles, CA on may 22, 1933 and he passed away on December 4, 1987. Arnold was married to Anita Lobel and was the father of two children.
